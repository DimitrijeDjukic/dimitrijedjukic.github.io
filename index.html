Progression Rules & Sport Return Roadmap

Upper Body Progression

Ramping sets: When you complete the top set cleanly with 1–2 reps still available, increase all weights by the smallest increment next session.

Straight sets: Complete all sets and reps cleanly → add 2.5 kg next session.

DB bench current: Starting at 10→12→13→14 kg/side. When 14 kg feels easy on set 4, move top set to 15 kg.

Barbell bench return: Weeks 4–6 when DB at 14 kg/side feels easy. Start at 40 kg total for sets of 10.

Lower Body Progression

Rule #1: No swelling = eligible to progress. Swelling = same weights, no exceptions.

Leg press: +5 kg on top set when all sets feel smooth.

Bulgarian split squat: +1 kg/side every 2 weeks. Tendons adapt 3× slower than muscles.

Deadlift: +2.5 kg/week maximum while technique is still developing.

Hip thrust: +5 kg when all ramp sets feel controlled.

Running + Sport Return

Now: 9 min · next target 9:30

15 min continuous = eligible for outdoor running

20 min + zero swelling 4 weeks straight = start direction-change jogging

Basketball / football return: Only after 20 min comfortable AND right leg single-leg press matches left leg weight. No shortcuts.
